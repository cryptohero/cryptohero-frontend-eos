Game Introduction

"EOSHero" is a block chain game with card collection based on "The Water Margin" which is one of China's four famous books. The total number of cards is 210,000, which has been written into smart contract. Every card is unique and every transaction can be checked in the chain. When the player purchases the card, he or she will owns the card and can freely trade and resell it. The player who collects 108 heroes of EOSHero will win a rich award.

Why do we do "EOSHero?"

The Little Raccoon Hero Card is an important memory after the eighties and nineties, carrying the happiness of a generation. We want to help everyone regain this happiness and beauty through block chain technology.So, today, let's call the old friends who bought instant noodles and gathered hero cards together that year to relive the joy of unpacking and the beauty of exchanging the desired hero card.

How to play "EOSHero"?

Start your journey in the nebula water margin world, you need:

Download nebula web wallet| Wallet tutorial

Recharge nebula coins to wallet ( available from exchange offices such as Hu bi and gate. io )

How to buy?

Click " Draw Cards Now" on the homepage, select the number of cards drawn, and complete the payment. Then you can see all the cards you own under " My Collection".

How to resell?

At present, we can join our wechat and other communities to communicate with other players in the group and exchange pricing on their own. In the future, we will open the trading market for players to trade more conveniently.

Card Issue

There are altogether 21,000 EOS hero cards, divided into 108 different roles, and each card is guaranteed to have a unique number by using the block chain intelligent contract technology.
